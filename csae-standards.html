Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: May 2026

Spotyroll is committed to maintaining a safe, lawful, and respectful platform for all users. We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including any material, conduct, or activity that harms, exploits, sexualizes, or endangers minors.

Spotyroll exists to help users discover websites, digital projects, and online experiences. Any attempt to use the platform to distribute, promote, link to, or engage with exploitative material involving minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate enforcement action.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Spotyroll strictly prohibits:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Content that exploits or sexualizes minors
  • Predatory behavior or grooming
  • Links to websites containing exploitative material
  • Attempts to recruit, manipulate, or endanger children
  • Any illegal activity involving minors
  • Content encouraging abuse, exploitation, or trafficking

Any user found violating these rules may have their content removed immediately and their account permanently banned without prior warning.

2. Reporting & Enforcement

We actively investigate reports related to child safety and take immediate action when violations are identified.

Enforcement actions may include:

  • Immediate removal of violating content
  • Permanent suspension of user accounts
  • Blocking access to harmful material
  • Reporting illegal content to relevant authorities
  • Cooperation with law enforcement investigations

Spotyroll reserves the right to preserve evidence and relevant account information when required for legal compliance, investigations, or safety purposes.

3. Cooperation With Authorities

Spotyroll complies with applicable child protection laws and cooperates with law enforcement agencies in cases involving suspected exploitation or abuse.

Where appropriate and legally required, reports may be submitted to organizations such as:

  • The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Relevant international or local law enforcement agencies
  • Child protection organizations

We may share necessary information to support investigations involving child safety threats or illegal exploitation.

4. User Responsibilities

All users of Spotyroll are responsible for ensuring that the content they submit, share, or promote complies with our policies and applicable laws.

By using Spotyroll, you agree not to:

  • Upload or link to exploitative or illegal material
  • Use the platform to target or harm minors
  • Attempt to bypass moderation systems
  • Engage in abusive or predatory conduct
  • Promote harmful or unsafe behavior involving children

Users are encouraged to report suspicious activity immediately.

5. How To Report Content

If you encounter content or behavior on Spotyroll that may involve child exploitation, abuse, or unsafe conduct involving minors, please report it immediately.

You can contact us through:

  • Email: hello@spotyroll.com
  • The in-platform reporting system (when available)

Please include relevant details such as usernames, links, screenshots, or descriptions to help us investigate efficiently.

6. Safety & Moderation

Spotyroll may use moderation systems, automated detection technologies, and manual review processes to identify potentially harmful or illegal content.

We continuously work to improve platform safety while maintaining a positive environment for creativity, discovery, and sharing.
